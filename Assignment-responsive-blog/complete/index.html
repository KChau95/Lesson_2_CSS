About Me

Kevin Chau is a multifaceted creator whose career bridges real-estate, technology, and entrepreneurship. By day he oversees thousands of rental units as a construction-savvy property manager, drawing on hands-on experience to spot under- or overpriced homes and even developing an algorithm that automates those valuations. After hours, you’ll find him deep in code—whether refining full-stack web apps in JavaScript, Python, or C#, experimenting with AWS cloud deployments, or building data-scraping tools with BeautifulSoup and Playwright. Kevin also runs Ritz Nails, a salon whose modern website he designed himself, and he’s constantly sketching new side projects—from a spider-identification app to a Discord bot for the iPhone game Last War. Equal parts analyst and storyteller, he’s now exploring film critique, eager to merge his eye for detail with a love of cinema.
